Based on the available information, Open Archives searches for related persons of the persons shown in the document, like children and parents.

By repeating this search for the found related persons, Open Archives can display a networked overview of relationships.

To use this feature you must be logged in and have a subscription.

Open Archives searches automatically, based on available records, for the children of couples.

When children are found, it is displayed with an = sign. With one click you can then view the list of children.

To use this feature you must be logged in and have a subscription.

The records you encounter on Open Archives and which are useful for your research, can be added to your list of favorite records. You can sort this list and when you search you will see immediately which records are already on your list of favorite records.

Favorite records can be downloaded in PDF format (ideal for printing) and GEDCOM format (ideal for importing into your family tree).

To use this feature, you need to login first.

If the information you find on Open Archives, is useful for your research, you can download it in PDF and GEDCOM format.

GEDCOM files can be imported into your family tree program. This way you do not need to type all the information in your family tree program.

Note: the files do not contain the scanned images!

To use this feature you must be logged in and have a subscription.
