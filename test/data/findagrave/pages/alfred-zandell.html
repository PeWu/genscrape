Mr. Zandell was a member of an old pioneer family of Thurston County. He wass survived by his widow, three daughters, and one son, all residing in Walla Walla; two brothers, Alexis and Oscar Zandell, and two sisters, Mrs. Olivia Moore and Mrs. Anna Gallagher of Olympia.
