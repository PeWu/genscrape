Limited information from an e-mail dated Jan. 2002 to my niece from Vernon B. York regarding our York family connections. Mr. York wrote that Thomas and wife "Franke" Mitchel York died 1840-1850 and were buried in this cemetery.

Their son William, b.ca 1816 Randolph Co., N.C., was my ggg grandfather. We do not have information on William's place of interment.
