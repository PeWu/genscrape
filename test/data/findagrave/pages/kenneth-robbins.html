Death: Feb. 2, 1972

Son of John Newell Robbins and Ann Ayers Francis

He married Edith Prescott Luckett 27 June 1916 in Burlington, Chittenden, Vermont, United States. They had one daughter, Anne Frances Robbins, who was later adopted by her stepfather Dr. Loyal Edward Davis and became known as Nancy Davis. She married Ronald Wilson Reagan in 1952.

Kenneth married second Patricia Brinkerhoff Cross in 1928.
